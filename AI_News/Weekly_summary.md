- **Agentic AI & Enterprise Productivity**: The SHRM‑Google‑Quantiphi report signals a shift from tool‑centric AI to autonomous agents that can execute end‑to‑end workflows, prompting firms to develop new governance and KPI frameworks.  
- **AI‑Enhanced Domain Models**: DeepMind’s WeatherNext 2 exemplifies how specialized AI models are moving beyond generic NLP to high‑impact verticals like energy trading, where speed and precision translate directly to revenue.  
- **Physical AI Investment**: CarbonBlack’s studio and partner appointment highlight growing capital appetite for AI that integrates tightly with hardware, reflecting a maturing ecosystem beyond cloud‑only solutions.  
- **AI‑Powered Cyber Threats**: The Akira ransomware expansion demonstrates that adversaries are increasingly embedding AI for adaptive phishing and rapid encryption, raising the bar for detection and response capabilities.  
- **Regional Market Volatility**: The Axios piece underscores that despite abundant funding, Asian AI startups face investor pressure to prove sustainable unit economics, suggesting a possible correction in the near term.
